About Vechoochira

Vechoochira is a serene village in the Pathanamthitta district of Kerala, surrounded by lush greenery, hills, and beautiful natural landscapes. It is known for its peaceful atmosphere and natural beauty.

Top Attractions

  • Sabarimala Pilgrimage Route
  • Gavi Ecotourism
  • Perunthenaruvi Waterfalls
  • Naranammoozhy Hills
  • Vechoochira Natural Beauty

Culture & Festivals

Vechoochira is known for its vibrant traditions, including the celebrations of Onam, Vishu, and temple festivals. The local culture reflects the heritage of Pathanamthitta with strong religious and cultural roots.
